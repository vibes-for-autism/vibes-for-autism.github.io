As soon as I put them in, it changed my world. You can hear everything you NEED to hear, and these block out most of everything you don't. I can hear conversations and music clearly, but a good chunk of the rest falls away into a quiet din. Amazing.

Whenever I get into a situation where there is too much noise, I am able to quickly put them in my ears because I always have them within reach. It's so much more tolerable shopping now because I can tune out all the overwhelming noise.

The freedom to hang out with my friends, listen to music, and not feel isolated are pivotal in maintaining my quality of life. The clear design allows me to wear them with any outfit, still wear earrings and give me the confidence to not wince when glasses are clinked in a toast of celebration. Right now I need my Vibes to live!
